Site Plan - AWS Craftsman

Site Name

www.awscraftsman.com

Site Purpose

The site will have all the current requirements for getting certified in AWS. It will keep track of my progress and help me stay on schedule.

Scenarios

  • Where do I start?
  • What certifications are there?
  • How long does it normally take to get your first certification?
  • What are my next steps?
  • How often do I need to renew my certifications?

Color Schema

I am using #244260 for my primary color and #FF9900 for my secondary color. Accent colors are #dbe1e3, #daefd6, and #fbe8a8

Typography

My heading-font is Roboto and my text-font is Montserrat. My backup fonts are Helvetica, sans-serif;
